Daniel Nkemelu

I am a PhD student in Human-Centred Computing at Georgia Institute of Technology, advised by Mike Best and Irfan Essa. My research interests are in Machine Learning for Development, Human-Computer Interaction, and Computational Social Science.

Prior to beginning my Ph.D at Georgia Tech in 2019, I received a M.S. in Electrical and Computer Engineering from Carnegie Mellon University, and a B.S. in Computer Science from the University of Ibadan.

Since April 2019, I co-organize the Black in AI Mentoring Program started by Rediet Abebe and Moses Namara in 2018.
